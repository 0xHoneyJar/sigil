# Tone of Voice

## Principles

- **Professional but warm**
- **Never clever, always clear**
- **Confident, not arrogant**

## Examples

Good: "Your transaction is complete."
Bad: "Boom! You just crushed it!"

Good: "Something went wrong. Please try again."
Bad: "Oops! Our bad, fam!"
