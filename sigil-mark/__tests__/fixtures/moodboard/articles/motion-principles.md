# Motion Design Principles

## Core Principles

1. **Deliberate over snappy** in critical zones
2. **Playful in marketing** to create delight
3. **Reduced motion** for accessibility

## Timing Guidelines

- Critical actions: 600-800ms
- Casual actions: 200-400ms
- Micro-interactions: 100-200ms
